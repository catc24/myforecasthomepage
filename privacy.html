SnowDay Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Overview

SnowDay is a weather app that shows forecasts and a snow day probability. We collect only what we need to provide these features and do not sell your data.

Information We Collect

  • Location (optional): If you grant permission, we use your device location to show local weather and forecasts.
  • Device token (push notifications): If you enable notifications, we store your device token to send alerts you choose to receive.

How We Use Information

  • To request weather data for your area from third-party providers.
  • To send snow day probability updates and alerts you opt in to receive.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services to provide weather data and alerts (for example, Weather.gov and Apple WeatherKit). These services may process your location to return weather data.

Data Retention

We keep device tokens only as long as needed to deliver notifications. You can disable notifications at any time.

Your Choices

  • You can allow or deny location access in your device settings.
  • You can enable or disable notifications in your device settings.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the information we process.

Children's Privacy

SnowDay is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available in the app or on our website.
